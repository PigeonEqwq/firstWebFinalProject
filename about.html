About the Club


THMSS Music Club has been cooperating with the school since we were founded and helped with a lot of music-related activities.
If you are interested in music, why not choose to join us?
The club is a big family. If you want to join us, we do not require you to be any sort of MUSIC MASTER or EPIC PRODUCERS.
The only type of people we are looking for are those who have an eager love on music.
Feel free to join us if you want to know more about music!
